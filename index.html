What is this app?

Argynguard is a minimalistic, offline security tool designed to verify human identity against AI impersonation. It uses strong, standard algorithms to generate ephemeral secrets that remain strictly on your device.


Beyond basic encryption, the system addresses physical coercion. Under duress, it can generate decoy secrets that appear mathematically indistinguishable from authentic ones. This provides a plausible layer of defense, allowing you to maintain privacy even when forced to unlock your device.
